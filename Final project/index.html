Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our FAQs. If you have any other questions you'd like answered please feel free to email us.

What is Notefi?

Notefi is an app that enhance your note-taking experience. With the easy-to-use program interface you can access your notes in 1 second and share them with your family, friends or colleagues.

How can I access Notefi?

Notefi is easily accessible through mobile app on smartphones and tablets, desktop app for MAC or Windows, or through the web app on any browser.

What's the best plan for me?

Depending on how many features you need, we offer 3 different plans: free, personal or team. check out the pricing page for more info.

Is there a money back garantee?

We are confident you will enjoy our app. If for any reason you're unhappy with your experience, you have a 30-day money-back guarantee to get reimbursed on any Notefi plan you choose.
